About PORTALHOPPER

Overview

A "story-driven" 2D JRPG–platformer about a lost squid navigating a fracturing multiverse, guided by a mysterious voice and forced to confront fate, and the cost of breaking an endless cycle.

Gameplay

Traverse intricate platforming levels, solve puzzles, and engage in turn-based battles with unique mechanics. Upgrade your abilities, discover hidden paths, and experiment with different strategies to defeat challenging foes.

Story

Splat, a small squid with no memory of his past, awakens in a fracturing world connected by unstable portals. Guided by a mysterious voice, he journeys through strange worlds and interdimensional markets in search of a way home. Along the way, Splat encounters powerful beings, ancient machines, and characters tied to a long-forgotten tragedy. As the truth behind the voice begins to unravel, Splat must decide whether to follow fate—or challenge the cycle holding reality together.
